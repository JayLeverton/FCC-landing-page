Frequently Asked Questions

What is a mechanical keyboard?

A mechanical keyboard typically uses spring activated switches in their keys, as opposed to rubber domes like a lot of other keyboards use. This gives the keys their signature clicky feel but switches can vary wildly in their sensitivity, sound, and feel.

Why should I use a mechanical keyboard?

A mechanical keyboard is typically faster, more durable, and more reliable than a keyboard that uses rubber switches. Mechanical keyboards also offer a tactile feedback that can increase your confidence and typing speed.

What switches are for me?

We offer a wide array of possible switches that you can choose from, whether you like loud tactile clicking, or a quieter typing experience.
